Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 8

Alumni Success Stories

Featuring successful graduates who talk about how your program prepared them for their next steps in life. This demonstrates the long-term impact of your educational model.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Lock phone orientation to Vertical (9:16).
  • • Audio Rules: Ensure the graduate is in a quiet space free of wind, background traffic, or crowd noise.
  • • The Statement: Instruct the graduate to look directly at the phone lens and record a 15-20 second statement detailing their name, current career or college, and how your program got them there.
  • • Supporting Visuals: Have them send you 2-3 photos or short video clips of them on their current campus, workspace, or throwback program files to overlay as B-Roll.

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
