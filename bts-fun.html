Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 9

Behind-the-Scenes & "Fun" Moments

Candid clips, like students telling jokes or staff preparing for a big event. These lighthearted videos foster a sense of belonging and community engagement.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Always hold the phone vertically (9:16).
  • • Audio Rules: Keep raw audio ON. Laughter, jokes, and voice punchlines are the focal points of this video.
  • • If doing Students Telling Jokes: Pull students aside during recess. Ask them to deliver a rapid joke. Capture the raw reaction laughter right after.
  • • If doing Staff Prep: Record the fun, unpolished reality—blowing up event balloons, laughing while moving tables, or drinking coffee before the rush.

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
