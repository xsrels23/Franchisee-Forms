Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 5

Virtual Campus & Facility Tours

A guided tour of your classrooms, playgrounds, and safety features. This helps prospective families feel comfortable with the environment before they ever step foot on campus.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Select based on distribution preferences (Vertical for reels, Horizontal for web embeds).
  • • Audio Rules: Speak loudly and clearly. Walk and talk in empty, quiet rooms, avoiding high-volume student hallways.
  • • Intro Clip: Stand by the building sign. Film yourself in selfie mode for 5 seconds saying: "Welcome to our campus tour!"
  • • B-Roll Integration: For every room you walk into, switch the camera around and take three steady 5-second b-roll shots of clean desks, security locks, or layout details.
