Objective 1

"Day in the Life" Student Journey

Follow a student’s daily routine to show families exactly what their child will experience. This builds familiarity with the daily rhythm and transitions of your program.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Phone Orientation: Always hold your phone vertically (9:16 vertical ratio).
  • • Audio Target: Film inside quiet rooms. Record any voiceovers inside a small carpeted space or closet.
  • • The 5-Second Rule: Stand completely still. Press record, count to 5 in your head, then press stop. Do not pan or wave the phone.
  • • Chronological Clips: You must capture 2 separate 5-second clips for each of the 4 milestones listed below.

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
