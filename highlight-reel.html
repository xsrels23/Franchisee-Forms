Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 4

Program Highlight Reels

A montage of high-energy moments from sports, STEM projects, and arts and crafts. These showcase a vibrant community spirit and the diverse extracurricular opportunities available.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Hold phone vertically (9:16).
  • • Audio Target: No voice audio required. Raw sounds will be muted and layered beneath a heavy rhythm track.
  • • Proximity Rule: Get physically close to the action! Walk directly up to subject spaces rather than pinch-zooming, which reduces resolution.
  • • Shoot Target: Capture a minimum of 3 short action clips for each category marked below. Focus on fast actions (ball entering hoops, paintbrushes striking canvases).

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
