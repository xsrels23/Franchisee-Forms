Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 10

Educational "How-To" for Parents

Short explainer videos addressing common parent concerns, like "Managing the Homework Environment" or "Building Study Skills". These position your brand as a helpful partner in their child's development.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Lock phone orientation to Vertical (9:16).
  • • Vocal Tone: Speak in a friendly, clear, authoritative yet warm tone. Minimize room echo.
  • • Tip Delivery: Record a staff member addressing the lens directly for 15 seconds outlining the tip (e.g., "Use a 20-minute physical desk timer").
  • • B-Roll Demonstrations: Shoot two 5-second action clips showing a student actually implementing the advice (e.g., looking at a desk timer, writing on a clean desk workspace).

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
