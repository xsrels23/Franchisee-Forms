Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 7

Enrichment Program Explainers

Detailed clips focusing on specific strengths, such as a specialized music department or a cutting-edge STEM lab. These attract families looking for specific academic or creative enrichment.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Always hold the phone sideways (16:9 horizontal ratio).
  • • The Presenter Hook: Film your host standing in the lab or studio. Have them deliver a 10-second vocal introduction directly to the lens.
  • • Action Close-Ups: Capture 3 highly detailed close-ups of specialized equipment in action (e.g., 3D printer nozzle, violin strings being tuned).
  • • Student Takeaways: Line up 2-3 students against a plain wall. Film them answering: "What is the coolest thing you've made in this room?" (Keep answers under 5 seconds).
