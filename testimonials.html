Brand Booster Media Order Pipeline
Objective 2

Parent & Student Testimonials

Authentic interviews where parents share their peace of mind and students discuss their favorite activities. These provide "social proof" that your program delivers real value and academic growth.

MANDATORY FILMING STANDARD

  • • Orientation: Check your final feed layouts. Use Vertical (9:16) for social platform posting, or Horizontal (16:9) for website homepages.
  • • Audio Rules: Find a silent indoor room away from foot traffic or wind. Stand 3 feet away from the subject for clear mic capture.
  • • Subject Framing: Lock the camera on a flat surface or tripod at eye level. Instruct the speaker to look slightly to the side of the camera lens, directly at you, rather than down the lens.
  • • Shot List: Upload 1 long, uninterrupted talking clip (Interview) + 3-4 separate action clips (B-Roll) representing their statements.

⚠️ Important: Please set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link" so our team can access your files.
